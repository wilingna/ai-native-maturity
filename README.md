## 🌍 English

### The Problem
Every consultancy has an "AI maturity model." They average six nice-looking scores into one number that makes leadership feel fine — and buries the one dimension that's actually killing the rollout. Averages lie. Chains don't.

### How it scores
Pick your level (L1–L5) on six dimensions. Your real maturity = the **minimum**, not the mean. The tool names your weakest link and points to the next move.

### The six dimensions
1. **Leadership & Direction** — does the boss actually use it, and can they say in one line what AI is *for*?
2. **Knowledge & Data** — is core know-how trapped in people's heads, or structured so AI can reach it?
3. **Workflow & Scenarios** — is AI embedded in a real process with someone accountable? (Doing it once = toy. In the SOP = a level up.)
4. **Talent & Capability** — one or two heroes, or frontline staff who use it daily?
5. **Incentives & Culture** — do employees feel AI is "digging their own grave" or "in my interest"?
6. **Governance & Resilience** — dare you scale? Permissions clear, failures caught, hand-off survivable?

## 🇨🇳 中文

### 问题
人人都有一套"AI 成熟度模型"，把六个好看的分一平均，凑出一个让领导安心的数字——顺手埋掉那个真正在拖垮落地的维度。平均分会撒谎，连环锁不会。

### 怎么定级
六个维度各选 L1–L5，真实水位取**最低**那一维，不取平均。工具会点出你的最短板，并指向下一步。

### 六个维度
一号位与方向 · 知识与数据 · 场景与流程 · 人才与能力 · 激励与文化 · 治理与韧性
（每一维都附"一眼判断口诀"，见自测工具。）
